How to make a Peanut Butter & Jelly

  1. Retrieve bread, jelly, peanutbutter.
  2. Using a table knife, spread jelly on topside of one of the pieces of bread.
  3. Get another clean knife and spread peanut butter on the topside of the other piece of bread
  4. Place the pieces of bread together... Making sure that the sticky sides face eachother.
